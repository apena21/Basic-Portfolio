About Me

I'm a native New Englander adjusting to Southern living in North Carolina. Though originally from the Boston area, I moved here after spending more than a decade in New York City. Boston Red Sox or Yankees? No comment. But I will say that that slower pace of Chapel Hill has its perks and the food around here is pretty good!

I'm pretty busy don't always get to enjoy my hobbies, but when I do have free time I love to exercise, especially swimming and spinning, reading, and learning new things. These days I'm focusing on learning how to code and how to speak German. I also love to eat, especially pizza, beer, and vegan chocolate cheesecake. I am not vegan or even vegetarian, but this particular vegan chocolate cheesecake recipe is awesome!
